# ESMa Taxonomy v1.0

## System for the Classification of Errores Sistemáticos en Matemáticas

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Introduction

The ESMa taxonomy organizes Errores Sistemáticos en Matemáticas through
a hierarchical structure that classifies recurring patterns of
mathematical reasoning according to their mathematical area and specific
topic.

Each ESMa record receives a unique code:

    ESMa-[Area]-[Topic]-[Number]

Example:

    ESMa-AR-JO-001

Where:

-   **ESMa** = Error Sistemático en Matemáticas (the original Spanish
    identity of the initiative).
-   **AR** = Mathematical area.
-   **JO** = Specific topic.
-   **001** = Sequential record number.

ESMa codes remain unchanged regardless of the repository language. Each
code represents the identity of a documented record and preserves
international traceability.
